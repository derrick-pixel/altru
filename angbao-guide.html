📋 Etiquette Do's & Don'ts

✅ Do

✓Use fresh, uncreased banknotes — old notes feel like an afterthought
✓Write your full name clearly on the back of the envelope
✓Choose amounts ending in 8 for good luck
✓Double the amount if attending with a partner
✓Give separately from tea ceremony ang baos (those are family roles)
✓Send a gift even if unable to attend — a smaller amount is still meaningful

❌ Don't

✗Use amounts containing the number 4 — it sounds like "death" in Chinese
✗Give odd numbers — they're associated with funerals
✗Leave the envelope blank — it can't be accounted for
✗Give below the seat cost — it puts the couple at a loss
✗Give coins — paper notes only for ang baos
✗Stress over the exact amount — sincerity matters most
💡 Weekend surcharge: Weekend dinners at premium hotels add 10–20% to banquet costs vs. weekday lunch. Adjust your ang bao upward accordingly.

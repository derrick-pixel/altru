Built on Trust & Transparency

Altru operates under Singapore's strictest charitable and data protection standards.

🏦

Gross Payment Rule

100% of your donation flows directly to the charity's account. Altru's platform fee is charged to the charity — never deducted from your gift.

🔒

PDPA Compliant

Your NRIC/FIN is collected solely for IRAS tax relief filing under Singapore's Personal Data Protection Act. It is never shared or sold.

📋

Licensed Fund-raiser

Altru subscribes to the Code for Commercial Fund-raisers under the Charities Act. Platform fees are held strictly under the 30% legal cap.

📊

Real-Time Transparency

Couples see a live breakdown of every gift received, charity amount raised, and tax deduction value — all in their personal dashboard.

⚖️

The 92 / 8 Rule

For every dollar donated, 92% goes entirely to the charity. Altru's 8% platform fee is charged to the charity — never deducted from the donation amount. This is far below the 30% cap set by Singapore's Charities Act, and well below the industry norm for commercial fund-raisers. See how we compare →
